Explore Rangamati: A majestic tourist destination in Bangladesh

By Go Sahabi

Tuesday | 27 July 2022

Share it

  • Facebook
  • twitter
  • Whatsapp
  • Email
  • Copy Link

Bangladesh has a larger monastery called Rajbana Vihar. It is situated in the Chittagong and Rangamati Hill tracts. There are more than 93 branches available in various locations across the nation, including some units in INDIA. Both in Bangladesh and other countries, Vihar is well-known. Due to the revered holy monk Banabhante, who passed away spiritually. Numerous tourists, pilgrims, worshipers, mediators, and visitors from around the country and beyond prefer to visit Rajbanavihar and take classes throughout the year. The Rajbana Vihara Complex is located on 33 acres of contoured land, encircled on all three sides by the magnificent Kaptai lake, in a peaceful cove beneath a lush grove. The Rajbana Vihara is connected to the Rangamati-Chittagong major road to the south by a road that passes through the gym.
